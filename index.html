About Me

I am an Economics PhD student at the University of Hawaiʻi-Mānoa (UHM), where I am also a Research Assistant at UHERO. In my time at UHM, I have held teaching assistant positions for introductory and intermediate undergraduate courses as well as lecturer positions for introductory undergraduate courses during the summer breaks.

Research Interests

The broad goal of my research is to tackle societal challenges from diverse perspectives. My passion involves advancing sustainable practices, with a specific focus on environmental and resource management. I am committed to exploring innovative strategies that develop and facilitate preservation of scarce resources for future generations.
